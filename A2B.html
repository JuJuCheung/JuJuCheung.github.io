About A2B

A2B is a subject that helps students build important digital knowledge and online safety skills. It teaches how to stay safe online, use technology responsibly, and make smart choices when working, communicating, and learning on the internet.

What Students Learn

Online Safety

Students learn how to protect personal information, use strong passwords, and stay safe when using websites and apps.

Digital Communication

Students explore how to communicate respectfully online and understand how messages, posts, and comments affect others.

Research Skills

Students practise finding reliable information online and learn that not everything on the internet is true or trustworthy.

Responsible Use

Students learn how to use devices, websites, and digital tools in a safe, sensible, and respectful way.

Main Topics

Staying Safe

Learning how to keep passwords secure, protect private information, and avoid online risks.

Smart Choices

Understanding how to make good decisions online and think carefully before clicking, posting, or sharing.

Respect Online

Exploring how to behave kindly and responsibly online, including how to deal with cyberbullying.

Trusted Information

Finding out how to check if information online is accurate, reliable, and safe to use.

Digital Confidence

Using technology with confidence while staying aware of dangers, responsibilities, and online rules.

Important A2B Skills

Password Safety

Using strong passwords and keeping account details private.

Reporting Problems

Knowing when to block, report, and tell a trusted adult if something goes wrong online.

Thinking Before Posting

Understanding that photos, comments, and posts can have lasting effects.
