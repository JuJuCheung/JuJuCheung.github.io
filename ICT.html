About ICT

ICT stands for Information and Communication Technology. It is about using computers and digital systems to store, process and share information.

ICT is important in school and everyday life because it helps people communicate, complete work, learn new skills and stay connected across the world.

Main Areas of ICT

Communication

Emails, messaging, video calls and social media help people communicate quickly.

Data Storage

Files can be saved on hard drives, USB sticks and cloud storage systems.

Networking

Networks connect devices so they can share information and resources.

Cyber Security

Protects devices, data and accounts from threats such as hacking and viruses.

How ICT Is Used

  • Education – students use ICT for research, presentations and online learning.
  • Business – companies use computers for communication, finance and records.
  • Healthcare – ICT helps store patient information and improve services.
  • Entertainment – streaming, gaming and media all use ICT systems.
ICT makes it easier to communicate, learn, work and solve problems in modern life.

Tools Used in ICT

Computers

Used to create documents, browse the internet and run software.

Smartphones

Useful for communication, apps and accessing information anywhere.

Routers

Connect devices to the internet and help create networks.

Cloud Storage

Lets users save files online and access them from different devices.

Why ICT Is Important

ICT is important because it helps people work faster, share information and solve problems. It is used in schools, businesses, hospitals and homes every day. Good ICT use combines communication, efficiency and safety.

Internet Safety Example

Internet safety is a very important part of ICT. People should use strong passwords, avoid suspicious links and never share personal information online. Safe ICT use helps protect users from scams, viruses and cyberbullying.

Topic

Internet Safety

Main Purpose

To protect users and information online

Main Tools

Passwords, antivirus and secure websites

Key Feature

Protection and awareness

Risks

Viruses, scams, cyberbullying and hackers.

Protection

Use strong passwords and think before clicking links.

Safety

Never share personal details with strangers online.

User Need

Safe and responsible internet use.

Advantages

  • Helps protect personal information
  • Reduces the risk of scams
  • Keeps devices safer from viruses
  • Encourages responsible online behaviour

Disadvantages

  • People may forget safety rules
  • Weak passwords can be risky
  • Unsafe websites can cause problems
  • Scammers can trick users easily
