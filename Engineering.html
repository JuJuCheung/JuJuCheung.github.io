About Engineering

Engineering is about designing, building and improving products, systems and structures. Engineers use science, maths and problem-solving to create solutions.

It is important in GCSE DT because it helps students understand how products are made strong, safe, useful and efficient.

Types of Engineering

Mechanical

Focuses on machines, moving parts and mechanisms.

Civil

Designs buildings, bridges, roads and large structures.

Electrical

Works with circuits, power systems and electronics.

Aerospace

Deals with aircraft, spacecraft and flight technology.

Engineering Projects

  • Bridges – designed to carry loads safely and efficiently.
  • Robots – combine mechanical parts, electronics and coding.
  • Vehicles – require structure, movement and energy systems.
  • Buildings – must be stable, safe and practical for users.
Engineering helps turn ideas into real solutions that improve everyday life.

Materials Used in Engineering

Steel

Very strong and used in buildings, bridges and machines.

Aluminium

Lightweight, strong and resistant to corrosion.

Plastic

Useful for lightweight products and insulation.

Concrete

Common in construction because it is durable and strong.

Why Engineering Is Important

Engineering is important because it solves problems and improves the world around us. It helps create transport, buildings, technology and systems that people use every day. Good engineering combines function, safety and innovation.

Bridge Engineering Example

A bridge is a good example of engineering because it must support weight, stay balanced and remain safe over time. Engineers test designs and choose suitable materials to make sure the structure works properly.

Project Type

Civil Engineering

Main Purpose

To carry people and vehicles safely

Main Material

Steel and concrete

Key Feature

Strength and stability

Forces

Tension, compression, torsion and shear.

Testing

Models and calculations are used before building.

Safety

Structures must handle loads and weather conditions.

User Need

Reliable and efficient travel.

Advantages

  • Helps people travel more easily
  • Supports transport and trade
  • Can last a very long time
  • Shows strong engineering skill

Disadvantages

  • Can be expensive to build
  • Needs maintenance over time
  • Weather can affect materials
  • Poor design can be dangerous
